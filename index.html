Who We Are

A foremost name in Bahraini construction.

Kadi One Constructions WLL — a subsidiary of the Kadi Group — is one of the most important and active construction companies in Bahrain. Established in 2017 and strengthened by the merger of Al Ayat Contracting & Trading, we have grown into a certified construction contractor known for quality turnkey delivery.

We are renowned for quality turnkey project construction, project management, a rigorous health & safety policy, and cost-effective delivery within agreed timeframes — guided always by our three core values: Quality, Integrity and Reliability.

  • Certified construction contractor in the Kingdom of Bahrain (CR 110333-1)
  • Turnkey delivery — from design and build through to handover
  • 250+ skilled workforce and 30+ engineers & supervisors
  • Uncompromising standards for health, safety & quality
