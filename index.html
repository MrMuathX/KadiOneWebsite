Who We Are

A Bahraini name built on quality and trust.

Kadi One Construction is the civil construction arm of the Kadi Group — a well-reputed Bahraini developer specialising in real-estate acquisition, development, construction, maintenance and property management. We turn ambitious plans into enduring structures.

From high-rise residential towers to infrastructure and bespoke developments, our teams combine modern engineering with local expertise to deliver properties that are attractive homes and sound investments alike — including Bahrain's first environmentally-friendly residential building.

  • Licensed & insured civil contractor in the Kingdom of Bahrain
  • End-to-end delivery: design, build, handover & maintenance
  • Sustainable, environmentally-conscious construction practices
